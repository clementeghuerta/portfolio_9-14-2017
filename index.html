Open About Clemente
Hello, I am Designer and Frontend Coder. I designed and coded this website to showcase my work.

I am a very hard-working, organized, and a detail-oriented person with great interpersonal skills who enjoys working both in a team as well as independently. My greatest values in a work-type setting consist of efficiency, productivity, and completing deadlines and tasks that exceed expectations. Also, I can proficiently describe visual design choices, and communicate those choices well both verbally and in a written format.

My process at the moment involves much research, wireframes, sketches, high fidelity and low fidelity mock-ups, prototyping, designing mobile first, using wireframes, creating user paths, and designing responsive web designs (RWD) with grids. Also, I have a passion in creating application (app) designs, and have studied Human Interface Guidelines (GUI) and Material Design. I enjoy working with UI/UX design and front-end development mentality and have been coding for 3+ years while using coding grid systems like Bootstrap, Zurb Foundation. And I have experience using WordPress. I am highly proficient with HTML5, CSS, CSS3, and have some jQuery experience as well.

I studied graphic design at California State East Bay and have been concentrating on UI Design for over a year. I was a multimedia/ web developer at College of San Mateo, and I attended Academy of Art University where I learned many different skills such as how to storyboard concepts and design 2D and 3D illustrations. Lastly, I aspire to be a part of a great team and be a very valued member of that team where I can use my skill-set to create good designs while also being very open to feedback for best practices.
